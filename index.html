About Us

Make your holiday memorable

A family run bed and breakfast built from the ground initially for lodging. SoSleepy Bed and Breakfast was founded in June 28 2019 in palapye, and will soon open in Letlhakane and Tsabong. We strived to offer our independent minded guests luxurious and quality accommodation. We value comfort and enjoyment for all guests. We are ideally located in Boikago Ward Palapye, next to rolling stone restaurant before the railway line.
